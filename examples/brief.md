# Brief: "Still Light"

## Product
Aria solitaire ring — 18k yellow gold band with a round brilliant-cut diamond.

## Campaign idea
A 15-second hero film for the Aria solitaire, built for Instagram and YouTube
pre-roll. The tone is quiet, patient, and expensive. No people, no text on screen.
The ring is the protagonist.

## Feeling we want
The kind of stillness you only get in an empty gallery just before it opens.
Warm, low, directional light. Deep blacks that swallow everything that isn't
the ring. A single slow breath from one shot to the next.

## Visual references
- Cartier "Clash" product film (macro on metal, no people)
- Apple "Shot on iPhone" product macros (extreme detail, studio lighting)
- Velvet-and-marble fragrance campaigns (moody, architectural)

## Must not
- No lifestyle footage, no hands, no faces.
- No on-screen text.
- No rainbow gradient lighting — we want one warm key light, controlled.

## Deliverable
One 15-20 second 16:9 MP4. Three or four shots. Crossfades between shots.
